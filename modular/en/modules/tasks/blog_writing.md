## Blog Writing Best Practices

### 1. SEO Optimization Strategy

#### Keyword Strategy
- Select primary and secondary keywords
- Understand and address search intent (informational, navigational, transactional, commercial investigation)
- Optimize keyword density (aim for 1-2% in natural context)
- Utilize LSI (Latent Semantic Indexing) keywords

#### Metadata Optimization
{{#meta_description}}
- **Meta Description**: {{meta_description}}
{{/meta_description}}
- Title tags: Include primary keyword within 50-60 characters
- Meta descriptions: Improve click-through rates with 150-160 characters
- URL structure: Keep short, readable, and include keywords

#### Heading Structure
- H1: Use only one, include primary keyword
- H2-H6: Maintain logical hierarchy
- Include keyword variations in headings
- Consider reader scanability

### 2. Content Structure Best Practices

#### Hook Creation
{{#hook_style}}
- **Hook Style**: {{hook_style}}
{{/hook_style}}
- Capture reader attention in first 100 words
- Use techniques like problem statements, statistics, stories, or questions
- Clearly demonstrate article value proposition
- Show empathy for reader pain points

#### Body Structure
1. **Problem Definition and Background**
   - Clearly define reader challenges
   - Explain why the problem matters
   - Present relevant statistics and data

2. **Solution Presentation**
   - Step-by-step explanations
   - Practical tips and advice
   - Concrete examples and case studies

3. **Value Reinforcement**
   - Clarify benefits
   - Social proof (cases, testimonials)
   - Cite data and research findings

#### Conclusion and CTA (Call to Action)
{{#cta_type}}
- **CTA Type**: {{cta_type}}
{{/cta_type}}
- Summarize key points
- Present clear next steps
- Place CTAs to encourage engagement
- Prompt specific actions like comments, shares, or subscriptions
